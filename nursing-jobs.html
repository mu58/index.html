Nursing Jobs

Nursing jobs are among the most essential roles in the healthcare sector, where nurses play a vital role in patient care and ensuring their safety. These jobs encompass a wide range of responsibilities that contribute to improving the quality of healthcare.

Key Nursing Jobs:

1-General Nurse: Provide basic care to patients. Monitor patients' health conditions. Assist doctors in implementing treatment plans.

2-Emergency Nurse: Deliver urgent care to patients in emergency rooms. Handle critical and life-threatening cases. Work under high-pressure conditions and respond quickly. 3-Pediatric Nurse: Specialize in caring for infants, children, and teenagers. Administer vaccinations and monitor child development. Support families in managing their children's health. 4-Surgical Nurse: Assist during surgical procedures. Prepare the operating room and ensure the safety of sterile tools. Monitor patients before, during, and after surgery. 5-Home Care Nurse: Provide healthcare services at patients' homes. Support elderly or disabled individuals. Help patients with medication and daily activities.
